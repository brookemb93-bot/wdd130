Costa Rica Immerse yourself in la pura vida on a group trip to Costa Rica. From its jungles to its coast, Costa Rica is more than just a destination, it’s a way of life. 2 Hours est. $25
Middlefork of the Salmon ID One of the world’s greatest whitewater rafting experiences, the Middle Fork of the Salmon River provides a true adventure trip of a lifetime. It carves a northeasterly route through the central Idaho backcountry, with hundreds of thrilling rapids. The Middle Fork passes through soaring canyons and thick spruce forests, offering a memorable adventure for beginner and expert rafters alike. 4 Hours est. $30
Weber River The Weber River is shaping up for great rafting in 2025. Weber Canyon has nice scenery and interesting geological formations with Class I – II and sometimes Class III rapids during peak runoff in June and early July. 3 Hours est. $40
Vjosa Albania Rafting in Vjosa river is by far one of the Top 10 Albania Adventure Tours! Join us in rafting one of Europe’s last free flowing gem. Vjosa river is still untouched and rich with beautiful canyons, decorated river sections and small islands. 5 Hours est. $50
New River Gorge The New River begins high in the mountains of western North Carolina, crosses Virginia, and enters West Virginia near its southernmost tip. It then heads north to join the Gauley River, and form the Kanawha River. In so doing, it manages to transect every ridge of the Alleghenies. Its name is actually a misnomer in that it may be one of the oldest rivers in the world. 5 Hours est. $75
